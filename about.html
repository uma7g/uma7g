Who I Am

Born deaf. Built different.

My name is Umang Pandya. I am a Senior 3D Artist, Game Designer, and Animator. I was born deaf — I cannot hear, and I do not speak. For most of my life, people assumed that silence would limit me. They were wrong.

In a field where creativity, precision, and collaboration are everything, I have delivered 30+ professional projects, contributed to 5+ shipped games, and built a career spanning 12 years — entirely through written communication and world-class craft.

My deafness did not slow me down. It sharpened my focus. While others are distracted by noise, I work in complete clarity.

My Mindset

Deaf people can do anything.

I do not share my story to ask for understanding. I share it because I want other deaf and hard-of-hearing people to see that the creative industry has space for us — and that clients who work with us get something rare: an artist who communicates with absolute precision because every word matters.

My silence is not a barrier. It is my discipline. Every 3D scene I build, every animation I deliver, every project I complete — it all says the same thing: ability has nothing to do with hearing.

If you are a deaf artist reading this — keep going. Learn the tools. Build your portfolio. The work will speak for you, and it is louder than any voice.
